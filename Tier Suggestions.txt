🏥 Subscription Tier Suggestions
TIER 1: STARTUP ($99-149/month)
For solo practitioners or small clinics (1-2 dentists, <200 patients)

Included Features:

1-2 dentist accounts + 1 receptionist seat
Patient management (limited to 200 patients)
Basic appointment scheduling & calendar
Patient record keeping (basic clinical notes)
Payment tracking (single currency)
Invoice generation & printing
Mobile-friendly dashboard
Basic reporting (monthly summaries)
Email reminders (limited)
5GB storage
What's restricted:

No staff/credential management
No dental chart tracking
Limited payment methods (cash only)
No SMS notifications
Community support only



TIER 2: PROFESSIONAL ($299-399/month)
For established clinics (3-5 dentists, 500-2000 patients)

Includes everything from STARTUP, plus:

5 dentist accounts + 3 receptionist seats
Unlimited patient management
Advanced appointment features (bulk booking, reminders)
Dental chart tracking (tooth conditions, treatment history)
Staff profile management with licensing/specialization
Multiple payment methods (Cash, Card, Bank transfer)
Advanced clinical notes & treatment records
Prescription management
Activity audit logs
SMS + Email reminders
Custom service categories & pricing tiers
Monthly financial reports & analytics dashboard
Secure document storage (50GB)
Phone/Email support
